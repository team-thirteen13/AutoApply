**Mobile nav focus trap (D-10):**
Modify src/components/landing/mobile-nav.tsx:
1. Import useFocusTrap from "@/hooks/use-focus-trap"

8. Pass triggerRef through from Navbar to MobileNav — add triggerRef as a prop on MobileNav and forward it to useFocusTrap
